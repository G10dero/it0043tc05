Musical Score Technique

Posted by Uzui's 7th Wife

As a Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps, Tengen Uzui is a very powerful and skilled combatant. He has displayed incredible physical feats of strength and speed, being seen as the second physically strongest and the fastest runner out of the Taisho era's Hashira. He could match the combat proficiency of both Daki and Gyutaro, both Upper Rank demons that have fought and killed numerous Hashira in the past. Tengen was also able to effortlessly decapitate Daki without her realizing it, to the point that he didn't see her as an Upper Rank demon at all.

One of the reasons why he can fend off an Upper Rank Demon is because of the technique he developed called Musical Score (譜ふ面めん ). Tengen developed this technique using his heightened sense of hearing to work in conjunction with his Sound Breathing, where he analyzes combat maneuvers and deconstructs the rhythm of his enemies' attacks into sound, reading them like sheet music to the point every fight becomes like a song. This allows Tengen to gain an understanding of his opponent's habits and tactics, as well as their blind spots, giving him the edge against them. While it takes a long time for Tengen to construct the score, once completed, Tengen was able to fight Gyutaro on even terms despite having lost his left hand, but due to the poison he was pushed to the limit and was unable to strike at the Upper Rank's neck.

Posted by Boltchu

We later learn that he specifically is looking for it because the doctor who gave him the treatment used the flower. But was there any point in using this flower for the plot?
